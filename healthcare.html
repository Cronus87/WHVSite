How to Enroll in Medicare

  • Visit a Services Australia office (Find nearest office)
  • Bring passport with entry stamp
  • Complete enrollment form
  • Receive temporary card immediately
  • Physical card arrives in 2-3 weeks
üí° What Medicare Covers:
  • GP visits (bulk-billed = free)
  • Emergency hospital treatment
  • Some specialist appointments
  • X-rays and blood tests
  • Limited dental through public system
‚ö†Ô∏è Medicare Doesn't Cover:
  • Ambulance (except QLD/TAS)
  • Dental (except emergencies)
  • Optical/glasses
  • Physiotherapy
  • Private hospital

üè• Health Insurance (Non-RHCA Countries)

‚ö†Ô∏è Mandatory: Working Holiday Makers from non-RHCA countries MUST have health insurance!

Overseas Visitor Health Cover (OVHC)

Cost: $50-150/month

Covers: Hospital, some GP visits, emergencies

Providers: Bupa, Medibank, NIB, Allianz

Waiting periods: Pre-existing conditions 12 months

Travel Insurance

Cost: $60-120/month

Covers: Emergencies, accidents, repatriation

Better for: Short stays, adventure activities

Note: May not cover work injuries

Finding a GP

  • Bulk-billing clinics: Free with Medicare
  • Private clinics: $70-100, claim back ~$40
  • Walk-in clinics: No appointment needed
  • Telehealth: Phone/video consultations
üìç Find doctors near you: Health Services Finder | White Pages Health | HotDoc (bookings)

Typical Costs Without Medicare/Insurance

  • Standard GP visit: $70-100
  • After-hours GP: $150-250
  • Home visit: $200-400
  • Specialist: $200-500
  • Emergency dept: $500-1000+
üí° Finding Bulk-Billing Doctors:
  • Search "bulk billing doctor near me"
  • Call ahead to confirm they bulk bill
  • HotDoc app shows bulk billing clinics
  • Medical centres more likely than private practices

üöë Ambulance Services

‚ö†Ô∏è Not Free! Ambulance costs $500-5000+ depending on distance

Coverage by State

  • QLD & TAS: Free for everyone
  • Other states: Must pay or have insurance

Ambulance Insurance Options

  • State ambulance membership: $50-100/year
  • Included in many health insurance policies
  • Some travel insurance covers it
üí° Alternative to Ambulance:
  • Uber/taxi to hospital for non-emergencies
  • GP home visits for urgent care
  • Call Healthdirect 1800 022 222 for advice

üè• Sexual Health

Free/Low-Cost Services

  • Sexual health clinics: Free STI testing
  • Family Planning clinics: Contraception advice
  • Bulk-billing GPs: STI tests, contraception

Emergency Contraception

  • Available at pharmacies without prescription
  • Cost: $15-30
  • Effective within 72 hours (best within 24)

Regular Contraception

  • Need GP prescription

  • Condoms: Widely available
