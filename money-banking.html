üìù Account Opening Process

Step 1: Online Application (Before Arrival)

  • Choose bank and account type
  • Complete online form with passport details
  • Provide Australian address (can be hostel)
  • Receive customer number
  • Note your temporary password

Step 2: In-Branch Verification (After Arrival)

  • Bring passport and arrival stamp
  • Provide Australian phone number
  • Verify address (hostel booking works)
  • Activate debit card (arrives in 5-7 days)
  • Set up mobile banking

üèß ATMs and Fees

ATM Networks

  • Your bank's ATMs: Always free
  • Other bank ATMs: $2-3 fee
  • Independent ATMs: $2.50-5 fee (avoid!)
  • Cashout at shops: Free with purchase

Foreign Card Fees

  • ATM withdrawal: $5-10 AUD + your bank's fee
  • Currency conversion: 3-5% markup
  • Balance inquiry: $2-4

Total cost: Can be $15-20 per withdrawal!

üö® Scams and Security

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Scams Targeting Backpackers:
  • Job payment scams: "Accidental" overpayment asking for refund
  • Rental deposit scams: Fake listings requiring upfront payment
  • Tax refund scams: Emails claiming you have ATO refund
  • Bank impersonation: Calls asking for login details
  • PayID scams: Fake payment confirmation screenshots

Protect Yourself

  • Never share your banking passwords or PINs
  • Banks will NEVER ask for your password
  • Verify PayID payments in YOUR banking app
  • Use unique passwords for each account
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Check ATMs for skimming devices
  • Cover PIN when entering

üí∏ Superannuation (Retirement Savings)

What You Need to Know

  • Employers must pay 11% on top of your wages
  • Goes into super fund (you choose or they assign)
  • Can claim back when leaving Australia (DASP)
  • 35% tax on withdrawal for working holiday makers

Recommended Super Funds

  • Hostplus: Low fees, good returns
  • Australian Super: Largest fund, reliable
  • REST: Good for retail/hospitality workers
üí° Important: Keep all super fund details! You'll need them to claim your money when leaving Australia. Average claim is $2,000-3,000.

üìä Tax and Financial Records

Keep Records Of

  • All payslips (digital or paper)
  • Bank statements
  • Rental receipts
  • Work-related expenses
  • Super fund statements
  • Group certificates/payment summaries
  • ABN invoices (if applicable)

Tax Rates (2024)

  • First $18,200: 0% (tax-free threshold)
  • $18,201 - $45,000: 19%
  • $45,001 - $120,000: 32.5%
  • Working Holiday Makers: 15% from first dollar
