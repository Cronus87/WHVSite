💰 Know Your Rights & Pay

Minimum Pay Requirements

▼

• Must earn at least Award wage (casual farm rate ≈ $28/hr)

• Piece-rate work must average the same hourly rate

• Receive weekly/fortnightly payslips showing hours, tax, super

Required Documentation

▼

• Employer must sign Form 1263 confirming each day worked

• Keep all payslips and bank statements as evidence

• Take photos of work sites and upload to Secure Wallet

Your Rights

▼

• Contact Fair Work Ombudsman if underpaid (free, confidential)

• Refusing unsafe work is legal – ask for proper PPE

• You have the right to proper breaks and safe working conditions

🎯 Applying for Second/Third Visa

Step 1: Prepare Your Evidence

▼

• Collect all payslips and bank statements

• Ensure Form 1263 or employment references are complete

• Organize documents by employer and date

Step 2: Submit Application

▼

• Apply online through ImmiAccount before current visa expires

• Upload all supporting documents

• Pay visa fee (check current rates)

Step 3: Wait for Decision

▼

• Processing time: few days to several weeks

• You can start immediately or later if outside Australia

• Check email regularly for updates
