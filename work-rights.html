üç∫ RSA - Responsible Service of Alcohol

Cost: $50-150

Required for: Bars, restaurants, bottle shops, venues serving alcohol

Valid in: State-specific (some states accept others)

Duration: Usually 4-6 hours online

Providers:

  • NSW: Liquor & Gaming NSW approved providers
  • VIC: VCGLR approved RTOs
  • QLD: Approved RTOs only
  • WA: Approved RTOs (in-person required)

Valid in: State-specific

Duration: 3-6 hours online

Note: Often bundled with RSA for discounts

üìã Workplace Rights Checklist

Your Employer MUST:

  • Pay at least minimum wage
  • Provide pay slips within 1 day of payment
  • Pay superannuation (11% on top of wages)
  • Provide a safe workplace
  • Not discriminate based on nationality
  • Give proper breaks (30 min per 5 hours)
  • Pay on time (at least fortnightly)
  • Keep proper records

Your Employer CANNOT:

  • ‚úó Keep your passport or documents
  • ‚úó Force you to live in specific accommodation
  • ‚úó Charge you for getting the job
  • ‚úó Pay below minimum wage
  • ‚úó Threaten visa cancellation
  • ‚úó Make illegal deductions
