  • Strict application process
  • Furnishing costs

üìã Viewing Checklist

Before Viewing

  • Confirm exact address and viewing time
  • Check public transport access
  • Research the neighborhood safety
  • Prepare questions about bills/rules
  • Have ID and references ready

During Viewing

  • Test all taps, lights, and power outlets
  • Check for mold, especially in bathroom
  • Test phone reception in all rooms
  • Look for signs of pests
  • Check locks on doors and windows
  • Ask about internet speed/provider
  • Clarify what's included in rent
  • Ask about house rules and guests
  • Take photos of any existing damage

‚ö†Ô∏è Rental Scams - Red Flags

üö´ NEVER:
  • Pay bond before viewing the property
  • Transfer money overseas
  • Pay via gift cards or crypto
  • Rent from someone "traveling abroad"
  • Accept "too good to be true" prices

Common Scam Tactics

  • Fake listings: Using photos from real estate sites
  • Pressure tactics: "Many people interested, pay now"
  • Overseas landlord: Can't show property, will mail keys
  • Upfront payments: Asking for bond via Western Union
  • Identity theft: Asking for too much personal info
‚úÖ Safe Payment Methods:
  • Bank transfer with proper receipt
  • BPAY to official real estate trust account
  • Credit card (for hostels/hotels)
  • PayID to verified accounts only

üìú Tenant Rights & Responsibilities

Your Rights

  • Quiet enjoyment of the property
  • Minimum 24 hours notice for inspections
  • Repairs completed in reasonable time
  • Bond lodged with government authority
  • Proper notice for rent increases
  • Itemized receipt for all payments

Your Responsibilities

  • Pay rent on time
  • Keep property reasonably clean
  • Report damage immediately
  • Allow inspections with notice
  • Not disturb neighbors
  • Pay for damages you cause

Bond Return Process

  1. Give proper notice when leaving (usually 2-4 weeks)
  2. Clean property to entry condition
  3. Attend final inspection
  4. Return all keys and remotes
  5. Complete bond claim form
  6. Receive bond within 14 days

Sydney

  • Budget suburbs: Ashfield, Marrickville, Parramatta
  • Beach areas: Coogee, Bondi (expensive but popular)
  • Avoid: Expensive CBD, far western suburbs

  • Budget suburbs: Brunswick, Footscray, Preston
  • Popular areas: St Kilda, Fitzroy, Collingwood
  • Avoid: CBD apartments (small and expensive)

Brisbane

  • Budget suburbs: West End, Woolloongabba, Annerley
  • Popular areas: New Farm, Fortitude Valley
  • Avoid: Flood-prone areas without checking history

Perth

  • Budget suburbs: Victoria Park, East Perth, Maylands
  • Beach areas: Scarborough, Cottesloe
  • Note: More car-dependent than east coast

üí° Money-Saving Tips

Negotiation Tactics:
  • Offer to pay several weeks upfront for discount
  • Negotiate if staying 3+ months
  • Ask about including bills in rent
  • Offer to do cleaning/gardening for reduction
  • Look for places during winter (lower demand)
Hidden Costs to Budget:
  • Electricity/Gas: $20-40/week per person
  • Internet: $15-25/week shared
  • Contents insurance: $5-10/week
  • Laundry: $4-8 per load if no machine
  • Transport to work: $30-60/week
