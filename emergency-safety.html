üî• Natural Disasters & Hazards

üî•Bushfires

Season: October to March (varies by region)

Apps: Fires Near Me (NSW), VIC Emergency, Emergency WA

Fire Danger Ratings:

  • Catastrophic/Code Red: Leave bushfire areas immediately
  • Extreme: Avoid travel to bushfire areas
  • Severe/Very High: Have evacuation plan ready

If caught in fire: Call 000, seek shelter in cleared area/building, stay low

üåäFloods

Risk Areas: QLD (summer), NSW coast, NT (wet season)

Never: Drive through floodwater - "If it's flooded, forget it"

SES Flood Help: 132 500

Monitor: BOM Weather app, ABC Emergency broadcasts

üåÄCyclones

Season: November to April (Northern Australia)

Categories: 1 (weakest) to 5 (catastrophic)

Preparation: Emergency kit, battery radio, secure accommodation

During: Stay indoors, away from windows, beware the eye

Rip Currents

If caught in a rip:
  1. DON'T PANIC - Stay calm
  2. DON'T swim against it (towards shore)
  3. Swim PARALLEL to the beach
  4. Raise arm and call for help
  5. Float and conserve energy

üöó Road Safety

Outback Driving Safety

  • Tell someone your route and expected arrival
  • Carry extra water (4L per person per day)
  • Never leave your vehicle if broken down
  • Hire/buy an EPIRB for remote areas
  • Check road conditions: 13 19 40
  • Watch for wildlife at dawn/dusk
‚ö†Ô∏è Wildlife Collisions: Kangaroos and wombats can cause serious accidents. Avoid driving at dawn/dusk in rural areas.

üöî Crime & Personal Safety

Staying Safe

  • Avoid walking alone at night
  • Keep valuables out of sight
  • Lock hostel lockers always
  • Don't leave bags unattended on beaches
  • Be cautious with new "friends" offering deals
  • Trust your instincts

If You're a Victim of Crime

  1. Ensure your safety first
  2. Call 000 if emergency, 131 444 if non-urgent
  3. Don't touch anything (preserve evidence)
  4. Get police report number for insurance
  5. Contact your embassy if passport stolen
üí° Safety Tip: Take photos of all important documents and store in cloud. Keep embassy contact details saved in phone.
