üè¶ Superannuation Claim (DASP)

Departing Australia Superannuation Payment

You can claim your super after leaving if:

  • Your visa has expired/ceased
  • You've left Australia
  • You're not an Australian/NZ citizen
‚ö†Ô∏è Tax on Super:
  • Working Holiday Makers: 65% tax rate
  • Other temporary residents: 45% tax rate
  • Average claim: $2,000-4,000 (after tax)

How to Claim

  1. Wait until visa expires AND you've left Australia
  2. Apply online via ATO website
  3. Need: Passport, visa details, super fund info
  4. Processing time: 2-4 weeks
  5. Money sent to international bank account
üí° Pro Tip: If you don't claim within 6 months of leaving, your super goes to ATO as "unclaimed money" - you can still get it but takes longer.

üìä Tax Return Process

Who Must Lodge

  • Everyone who worked in Australia
  • Even if leaving before June 30
  • Deadline: October 31 (following tax year)

What You Need

  • PAYG summaries from all employers
  • Bank interest statements
  • Receipt for work-related expenses
  • Private health insurance statement
  • myGov account access

Expected Refund

  • Worked full year: $500-2,000 typical
  • Worked part year: $200-800 typical
  • Processing time: 2-12 weeks
üí° Options:
  • Lodge yourself via myGov (free)
  • Use tax agent (costs $50-150)
  • Backpacker tax services (avoid high fees)
