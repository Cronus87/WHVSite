üöó Car & Van Options

Pros: Freedom, can sleep in it, resell value

Cons: Registration, insurance, maintenance costs

Best for: 3+ months travel, regional work

‚ö†Ô∏è Required Costs:
  • Registration (rego): $300-800/year by state
  • CTP Insurance: $400-600/year (mandatory)
  • Roadworthy certificate: $150-300
  • Comprehensive insurance: $600-1200/year

Car Rental

Cost: $30-60/day (economy)

Pros: No maintenance worry, newer cars

Cons: Expensive long-term, age restrictions

Best for: Short trips, under 25s pay more

Cost: $50-150/day

Cons: Very expensive, fuel costs high

Best for: 1-4 week road trips

Relocation Deals

Cons: Strict time limits, specific routes

üöó Driving in Australia

Essential Rules

  • üöó Drive on the LEFT side
  • üö® Give way to the RIGHT (unless signs say otherwise)
  • ‚õî Speed limits strictly enforced (heavy fines)
  • üì± Mobile phone use while driving = $500+ fine
  • üç∫ Blood alcohol limit: 0.05% (0.00% for some licenses)
  • üí∫ Seatbelts mandatory for all passengers

License Requirements

  • Can drive on overseas license for 3-6 months (varies by state)
  • Must carry International Driving Permit if not in English
  • Some states require license translation
  • Consider getting local license if staying longer
‚ö†Ô∏è Unique Australian Road Rules:
  • Hook turns in Melbourne CBD (turn right from left lane)
  • School zones: 40km/h during school hours
  • Must slow to 40km/h passing emergency vehicles
  • U-turns banned at traffic lights unless signed
  • Headlights on when wipers required
